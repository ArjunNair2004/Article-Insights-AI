How Does Article Insights AI Work?

Our platform uses advanced machine learning models trained on thousands of real web articles to predict what makes content succeed. When you submit your article details, our AI analyzes:

  • Content structure (length, keywords, self-references)
  • Rich media (images, videos, media quality)
  • Publishing strategy (category, channel, best days/times)

What Do the Predictions Mean?

We forecast the expected share count for your article using a specially trained regression model. At the same time, a second model classifies articles as “Viral” or “Not Viral,” based on patterns from high-performing content.

Why does this work? Our AI looks at thousands of factors—just like top platforms and media companies do—to give you actionable, personalized feedback before you publish.
